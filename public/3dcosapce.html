Introduction

3D Collaborating Space is a virtual reality tool for designer to display, alter, simulate usage, and especially communicate on details about 3D modeling.

The concept is intended to incorporate rapid design methods to customized 3D design.

This project was associated with HTC VR creative team, with concepts presented to creative manager on each stage of the design process.

Role

I worked as a UX designer, collecting qualitative data of industrial designer workflow, created user journey maps and personas.

Project advised by Chun-Cheng Hsu, Graduate Chair of the Institute of Applied Arts at the National Chiao Tung University in Taiwan.

Time

October 2019 - December 2020

Method & Tool

Method: Contextual Inquiry, Persona, Storyboard, User Journey Mapping

Tool: Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects
