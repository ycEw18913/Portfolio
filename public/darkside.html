Darkside

Unreal Engine Development

Introduction

Darkside is an interactive game designed to illustrate the fall of the human nature. Every time we compromise on our darkside, we lose our humanity bit by bit until we completely lost in the dark.

Role

I worked both as a game developer and designer. I used Unreal Engine to create the whole experience, packaging it into .exe file to work with Microsoft Windows.

Time

November 2019 - Jan 2020

Tool

Unreal Engine, Adobe Fuse, Quixel Megascans

Background:

The idea of a parallel universe is always fascinating. Tons of artworks were based on this concept. We usually try to illustrate it by creating "portals" between worlds; however, what's truly interesting is people try to use this concept to demonstrate a new world, with different social structure, different nature, or even different physics, which gives us the freedom to reconstruct "everything" from the essential, unleashing the power of imagination.

"Every time you compromise yourself, you lose a piece of humanity." Our dark side has always been haunting us, and staying with us no matter what. However, the true demon is not us with our dark side but embracing it.

Concept:

The interactive game combines ideas above, illustrating our mind struggle with parallel universe. Our darkness and our integrity are just like two worlds, while the former keeps luring us from time to time. We might make wrong decisions, but there's still a chance to come back. However if we succumb to our darkside, it could engulf us forever.

  • 「場景切換」的感受衝擊，勾起好奇心與穿梭的探尋。
  • 在不同世界中的摸索與互動，建構對環境的發掘。
  • 藉由平行世界的概念，詮釋黑暗面始終與我們共存；然而一旦妥協將無法回頭。

Environment Design:

  • 兩個一模一樣的露天室內舞廳場景，卻有著截然不同的感受。
    • 場景 A：微光下伴隨音樂可見舞者舞動的剪影，散佈神秘又優雅的氣息。
      • 舞台火花噴發的粒子特效。
      • 在舞池各處，有著不同舞姿的舞者。
      • 舞廳的動態燈光 (BP_Spotlights)。
      • 掛墜般吊在天空的發光物體 (Emmisive Material)。
    • 場景 B：陰暗殘破，暗處隱藏著未知的事物。
      • 霧濛、黑紅配色的恐怖氣氛 (Post Process Volume, Point Light, Spotlight)。
      • 地面燃燒、煙霧的粒子特效。
      • 散落一地的碎片，製造髒亂的氣息 (Foliage)。

Act / Interaction Design:

  • 透過「穿梭點」在兩個不同的世界的「同一位置」跳躍，創造即時的視覺與互動變化 (Teleport Function)：
    • 場景 A：玩家可跟隨舞池中音樂舞蹈，融入創造的神秘氛圍。
      • 伴隨音樂，環繞舞池與其他舞者共舞 (Animation Graph)。
      • 調整跳舞時移動的速度，動態模糊下創造不同的舞動視覺 (Animation Graph)。
    • 場景 B：追著人跑的恐怖黑影，只在些微的照明下看見懾人的樣貌。
      • 追著人跑的不明生物 (BP_Charater, Pawn Sensing)。
      • 主角慢速與快速跑動的切換 (Animation Graph)。
      • 發光的穿越門象徵僅存人性的召喚，在金入黑暗世界後
