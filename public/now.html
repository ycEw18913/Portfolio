YouBike Today: User Experience & Usability Evaluation

Working with product manager of YouBike Today to assess needs and behavior of target users and evaluate the usability and functionality of interactive systems using a variety of UX research methods. The project will include:

  • Mapping the interaction flow (a diagram of the system's reaction to user input).
  • Conducting contextual inquiry to understand target users’ needs, tasks, contexts, and goals.
  • Creating personas and scenarios for the system.
  • Surveying users for their opinions and experiences.
  • Comparing the features and usability of your system with comparative systems.
  • Using checklists and “heuristics” to evaluate the system from several points of view.
  • Doing user testing of typical tasks on the system.
  • Incorporating new and emerging evaluation methods.
