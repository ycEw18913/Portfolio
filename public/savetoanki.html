Introduction

SaveToAnki is an English Vocabulary Learning tool developed as a Google Chrome Extension. It was designed to automate the learning process of vocabulary we encounter on daily basis.

It automatically creates flashcards in AnkiDroid directly from words we look up on Google Translation. The flashcard includes translation, pronounce, and example sentences for better learning experience.

About AnkiDroid

AnkiDroid is a flashcard app creating spaced repetition reviews by considering forgetting curve.

SaveToAnki eliminates the time creating flashcards one by one, which significantly increases efficiency.

Role

Features

Auto Search: By pressing Shift on the keyboard, the tool will turn on the Google Translation tab and show the result.

Auto Log: The tool generates a button on Google Translation tab; by clicking it, a flashcard contained formatted information of the word saved into AnkiDroid.
