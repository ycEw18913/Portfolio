Introduction

This is an online design practice aiming to create a expense tracking service for groups, especially like startups, to automate and simplify expense reporting.

In this practice, problem and basic design requirements were provided in advance.

This design project is associated with the Udemy course, The Complete Sketch 5 Course.

Role

I worked as a UI/UX designer on this personal project from the initial idea, through Wireframing, to Prototyping and, finally, the deployment of assets to developers. I created designs including functional map, low-fidelity prototype, styleguide, mobile and web interface, and landing page.

Time

July 2019 - August 2019

Method & Tool

Method: Wireframing, Prototyping, Styleguide, Logo design, UI design

Tool: Sketch, Overflow, Invision, Zeplin, Notion

Design Process

Problem

When it comes to group expense like startups, common problems below are usually encountered:

  • Paper receipts are easy to lose and difficult to document.
  • No real-time monitoring causes teams to rely on delayed bank statements.
  • No department spending breakdown and no spending limits prevent teams to spend smarter.

Ideation

So here comes Spendo, the idea to change the way we manage corporate expenses by using a virtual card and automate company expenses. It would be:

  • InstantaneousÂ - Realtime sharing of budget and expenses
  • CohesiveÂ - Organized and cohesive tracking reports
  • AccessibleÂ - Supported among mainstream platforms
  • ReliableÂ - Reports are backed by a company card or a receipt

Overall, the core value this product should achieve is to answer the question:

How much money has been spent by whom at what time and how much is left?

There are three basic design requirements:

  • Firstly, this design should include expense entries labeled with time, place and category.
  • Secondly, it should include a credit card management feature.
  • Thirdly, it should have a receipt uploading interface.

Styleguide

Set up a style guide before the UI design to make sure the consistency of appearance, simultaneously increase the design efficiency.

Logo Design & Iconography

Created a logo graphic to represent Spendo brand, with different versions to suit different backgrounds. Designed the icons to visualize the actions.

Mobile App Design

The mobile app provides users information such as remaining budget, transaction history. It allows users to input expenses. A corporate card is connected to their profile, allowing for real-time updates on the users' balance. Those who pay in cash will have credentials updated by scanning their receipts. As purchase time, location, and category are provided, information is organized and is accessible upon request.

Followed the style guide for colors and text. Created symbols for all the reusable UI components such as menu and expense items to maintain consistency.

Web App Design

The web app was created to monitor and analyze member spending activity. Expense reporting of every individual within the team is clearly listed with necessary information such as category, time, amount, and the option to approve.

Home Page Design

Additionally, created a website for the company to sell their expense management app, showcasing the benefits of automated expense tracking, flexible use of virtual card, and positive feedback of the product.
