YouBike Today: Usability Evaluation & Redesign

Introduction

YouBike Today is a mobile tool designed for YouBike, the most popular bicycle sharing service in Taiwan.

In this project, we're going to apply multiple UX research method to evaluate the design of the tool, and expecting to present the findings and recommendation.

Role

I'm currently working as a UX designer with a diverse team from multi-disciplinary backgrounds in association with YouBike Today on this project.

Time

March 2020 - Ongoing

Method & Tool

Method: Contextual Inquiry, Persona, Scenario, Comparative Analysis, Survey, Heuristic Evaluation, User Testing

Tool: Sketch, Figma, Adobe Xd, Overflow, Notion
